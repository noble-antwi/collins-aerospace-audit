## Assignment Overview

Analysis of how COBIT 2019 governance and management objectives should evolve to address artificial intelligence and next-generation technologies, proposing enhancements to at least 10 objectives across all five domains (EDM, APO, BAI, DSS, MEA).

## Research Foundation

- KPMG "Future of Audit" insights on AI transformation
- COBIT 2019 Governance-Management-Objectives-Practices-Activities framework
- Current AI implementation trends in enterprise environments
- Industry best practices for AI governance and risk management
